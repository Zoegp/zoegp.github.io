Measure leaf data

So much data. Absolute as well as relative! Take a photo at any angle, and the app will correct the skewed perspective to give you a more accurate measurement.

Draw in margins

Leafbyte allows you to draw in margins that have been removed by herbivores. The freehand drawing button allows you to recreate any leaf shape.

Saves your data!

LeafByte automatically saves your data for you in google drive or on your phone. If you do not have the files app, data sheets can be accessed through itunes. LeafByte will form a file in which it will store both the pictures and a spreadsheet containing date and time stamps, latitude and longitude, sample number, leaf area, leaf area consumed and percent leaf area consumed.

Measure any leaf shape

Leafbyte works for a wide variety of leaf shapes, including both simple and compound leaves. You can eaven measure petals and flat flowers. However, it may be difficult to use for plants with needles or lacy leaves like those in the apiaceae family.

Reads any barcode

Leafbyte will read any standard linear or 2D barcode, record it, and save it in your data sheets.

Measure non-distructivly

With a clipboard and matte acrylic sheet, it's easy to measure herbivory non-distructivly in the field.
